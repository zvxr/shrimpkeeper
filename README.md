
Proof of Concept
- Explore your shrimp tank by swimming around exploring, collecting items, and
  breeding shrimp. Manage tank parameters for healthy shrimp, provide them with
  plenty of food by adding plants and nutrients, and discover new colors and
  features as shrimp reproduce!
- You are a diver (8x8 with 4 frames), with a freedom to swim about the tank
  and interact with different things.
- Collect coins which randomly appear as more are picked up, and use them in
  the shop.
- Use 'x' to swim, left/right arrow keys to navigate, and 'z' to toggle action
  items.
- Navigate across multiple screens that are unlocked as you play. The top of
  the screen is always reserved for a status bar, which shows you your money,
  tank parameters (pH, ammonia, kH, gH, TDS, stability)
